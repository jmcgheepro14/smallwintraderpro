# Legal Checklist

This is not legal advice. Have an attorney review before public launch.

Required pages:
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Risk Disclaimer
- No Financial Advice Disclaimer
- Subscription Terms
- Refund Policy
- Support Contact Page

Use safer wording:
- educational trade setup
- practice trade
- paper trading
- example entry zone
- risk-management plan

Avoid:
- guaranteed profits
- high probability profit
- make money daily
- buy now
- sell now
- sure win
- risk free
