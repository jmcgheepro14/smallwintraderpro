# App Store and Play Store Notes

Apple needs: Apple Developer Account, Privacy Policy URL, Support URL, subscription disclosure, In-App Purchases, clear no-financial-advice wording, and no misleading income claims.

Google Play needs: Google Play Developer Account, Privacy Policy URL, support email, Google Play Billing, Data Safety form, and clear cancellation instructions.

Safer positioning: Beginner trading education and paper trading practice.

Avoid positioning: AI guaranteed stock signal app.
