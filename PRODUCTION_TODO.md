## Payments
Use:
- Apple In-App Purchases for iOS subscriptions
- Google Play Billing for Android subscriptions
- Stripe Checkout for web subscriptions

Do not collect raw credit card info yourself.

## Legal
Have an attorney review:
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Risk Disclaimer
- No Financial Advice Disclaimer
- Subscription Terms
- Refund Policy

## App Stores
Avoid claims like:
- guaranteed profit
- high probability profitable trade
- make money daily
- buy now
- risk free

Use:
- educational setup
- paper trading
- example trade plan
- risk-management practice
